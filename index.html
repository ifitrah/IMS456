About Me
Hi, I'm Ilham Fitrah Razali
Student of IM244 based in Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), Campus Rembau, Negeri Sembilan

I currently lives in Sendayan. I am 21 years old, and my date of birth is on 30th November 2001. I am the only child in my family. YES! I must admit that it is kinda bored when you're most of the time will be alone. But, I managed to overcome the boredsome.

As a student, I am most likely having an issue with understanding syllabus. The struggle to read the same chapter repeatedly until we achieve the target to understand the whole content. But, that is life right? What kind of life we live in if there are no challenges coming towards us? A fairytale? NO! This is not a Disney movie character, where there are always a happy ending at the end of the story.

Student's life is fun and also challenging. We get to know new friends, or even new enemies. Some of us might think that studying is 'a piece of cake', and some of us might think studying is useless because we'll ended up being a jobless person due to the economy nowadays. Personally, for me, studying is for someone who have an interest to gain more knowledge about things that are new to us. So, it depends, what kind of a person you are? Go and ask yourself!
